Coarse Offer

Information Technology

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology is a computer-related program that prepares students on how to install, operate, develop, maintain, and administer computer applications. It also equips students with the basic ability to conceptualize, design, and implement software applications. This program is for students who are looking to have a career as an Applications Developer, Web Developer, Network Engineer, Web Administrator, Database Administrator, etc..

Teacher Education

The Bachelor of Elementary Education is a four-year degree program that combines theory and practice designed to prepare students for teaching in primary schools. Major offered in this college:
Generalist

The Bachelor of Secondary Education is a four-year degree program that combines theory and practice designed to prepare students for teaching in secondary schools. Majors offered in this college: English Mathematics Values Education Science

Business Administration

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) program focuses on developing students' critical decision-making skills for strategic and executive roles in the global business environment. The program offers majors in Marketing Management, Financial Management, and Human Resource Development Management. Marketing Management: Designed to equip students with knowledge and skills for effective marketing and sales strategies. Covers topics such as sales management, branding, distribution, e-commerce, retailing, and corporate social responsibility. Ideal for those pursuing careers in marketing, advertising, and research. Financial Management: A program emphasizing analytical skills and competencies for sound financial decision-making. Topics include financial analysis, investment strategies, economics, banking, and commercial planning. Geared towards students aspiring to work in the banking and finance industries. Human Resource Development Management: Integrates employment issues with business management, focusing on workforce organization, personnel training, recruitment, compensation, job analysis, and legal provisions. Addresses principles and theories of employment, personnel training, and skills development. Suited for those interested in careers in recruitment and human resources.
