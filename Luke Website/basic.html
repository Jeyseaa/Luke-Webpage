About

The Assumption College of Davao's Junior High School Program is a dynamic and nurturing community deeply aligned with its Vision, Mission, and Goals (VMG). Rooted in a profound love for the country, the program aims to foster a community where learners and teachers instill respect for humanity and a commitment to selfless service. Through a blend of academic and co-curricular offerings, the focus is on holistic learner development, nurturing creativity, critical thinking, and a deep living faith. The program strives to equip learners with essential knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes to navigate life's challenges, all while promoting active community engagement. Join the program to be part of a transformative educational journey that prepares learners to serve and make a meaningful impact on society.
Join us in the Assumption College of Davao's Elementary and Junior High School Programs, where we prepare learners to serve and make a meaningful impact on society.
